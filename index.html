Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, veggies, meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Mix eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil over medium heat.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour eggs, tilt pan for even coating.
  4. Add fillings: Sprinkle chosen ingredients before folding.
  5. Fold and serve: Cook another minute, then serve.
